Privacy Policy

Claude Off-Peak Timer · Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Claude Off-Peak Timer ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps users track off-peak usage windows for Claude AI during Anthropic's March 2026 promotion. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, IP address, etc.)
  • No browsing history or web activity is tracked
  • No analytics or telemetry data is sent to any server
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No data is shared with third parties

Local Storage

The Extension stores a single preference locally on your device using Chrome's storage.local API:

  • Notification preference — Whether you've enabled or disabled notifications (a boolean value: true/false)

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the extension.

Permissions

The Extension requires the following browser permissions:

  • alarms — To schedule periodic checks for off-peak/peak transitions and send timely notifications
  • notifications — To display desktop alerts when off-peak hours begin or peak hours are approaching
  • storage — To save your notification preference locally on your device

Timezone Detection

The Extension uses your browser's built-in Intl.DateTimeFormat API to detect your local timezone. This is done entirely within your browser — no timezone or location data is transmitted externally.

External Connections

The Extension makes no network requests. It operates entirely offline after installation. The only external link is the "Open Claude" button which opens https://claude.ai in a new tab when you click it.

Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since no data is collected, significant changes are unlikely.
