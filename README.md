# ⚡ Claude Off-Peak Timer — Chrome Extension

Never miss Claude's **2× off-peak usage** window during the March 2026 promotion.

## Features

- **Live Status** — Instantly see if you're in off-peak (2×) or peak (1×) hours
- **Auto Timezone** — Detects your timezone and converts ET schedule to local time
- **Countdown Timer** — Real-time countdown to the next off-peak/peak transition
- **Weekly Schedule** — See your full week's peak/off-peak schedule at a glance
- **Notifications** — Get alerts when off-peak starts and 5 min before peak begins
- **Promo Progress** — Track how many days remain in the promotion

## Installation (Developer Mode)

1. Download/unzip this folder
2. Open Chrome → go to `chrome://extensions/`
3. Enable **Developer mode** (toggle in top-right)
4. Click **Load unpacked**
5. Select the `claude-offpeak` folder
6. The ⚡ icon appears in your toolbar — click to open!

## How It Works

**Promotion period:** March 13–27, 2026

| Time | Weekdays | Weekends |
|------|----------|----------|
| 8 AM – 2 PM ET | Peak (1× normal) | Off-peak (2×) |
| All other hours | Off-peak (2×) | Off-peak (2×) |

The extension automatically converts these times to your local timezone.

## Eligible Plans

Free, Pro, Max, and Team plans. Enterprise plans are excluded.

## Permissions

- `alarms` — For scheduling notification checks
- `notifications` — For off-peak/peak alerts
- `storage` — For saving your notification preference
